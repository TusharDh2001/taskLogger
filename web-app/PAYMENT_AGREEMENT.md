## 1. Introduction

This Payment Processing Agreement ("Agreement") outlines the terms and conditions for payment processing services for POGO premium subscription features. This agreement is between POGO ("Service Provider") and users subscribing to premium services ("Users").

## 2. Services Covered

### Subscription Tiers

#### Free Tier
- **Price:** $0/month
- **Features:** Basic task management, calendar integration, notifications
- **No payment required**

#### Pro Tier
- **Price:** $4.99/month or $49.99/year
- **Features:** All free features + advanced analytics, priority support, custom categories
- **Billing:** Monthly recurring or annual

#### Enterprise Tier
- **Price:** Custom ($99+/month)
- **Features:** All Pro features + team collaboration, API access, SSO
- **Billing:** Custom billing arrangement

## 3. Payment Methods Accepted

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- Debit cards
- Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Bank transfers (for Enterprise)

## 4. Billing Terms

### Automatic Renewal
- Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing period
- You will receive email reminder 7 days before renewal
- You can cancel anytime from account settings
- No refunds for early cancellation

### Billing Date
- Free tier users: N/A
- Pro tier users: Charges on the same calendar date each month
- Annual subscribers: Charged annually on subscription anniversary

### Currency
- Pricing is in USD
- Currency conversion applied automatically for non-USD card holders
- Rates determined by payment processor

## 5. Payment Authorization

### What You Authorize
By subscribing to POGO premium, you authorize us to:
- Charge your payment method for the subscription fee
- Store your payment information securely
- Process recurring charges per billing schedule
- Retry failed payments up to 3 times over 15 days

### Payment Information
- We do NOT store full credit card details
- Payment information stored with PCI-compliant processor (Stripe/PayPal)
- Sensitive data encrypted with industry standard protocols

## 6. Pricing and Changes

### Current Pricing
Pricing subject to change with 30 days written notice. Changes will NOT apply to current billing cycles.

### Price Increase Notice
- We will notify you via email 30 days before price increase
- You can cancel before price increase takes effect
- Continuing service means acceptance of new price

### Tax
- Applicable sales tax added based on billing address
- Tax liability depends on state/country laws
- Tax amount itemized on invoice

## 7. Failed Payments

### Retry Policy
If payment fails:
1. **Day 1:** Automatic retry
2. **Day 7:** Second retry with notification email
3. **Day 15:** Final retry
4. **Day 16:** Service suspension if unpaid

### Re-enablement
- Service restored within 24 hours of successful payment
- No data loss during suspension
- Renewal date does not change

### Notification
- Email notification sent for each failed attempt
- Instructions provided to update payment method
- Contact support for assistance

## 8. Refunds and Returns

### Refund Policy
- **30-Day Money Back Guarantee** - Full refund for first month if unsatisfied
- **Annual Subscriptions** - Prorated refund within 30 days of purchase
- **No refunds** on partially used months after 30-day period

### Refund Process
1. Contact support@pogo.app with refund request
2. Provide subscription details and reason
3. Refund processed within 5-7 business days
4. Original payment method credited

### Non-Refundable
- Promotional or discounted subscriptions
- Annual subscriptions after 30-day period
- Service credits or promotional codes

## 9. Cancellation

### How to Cancel
1. Account Settings → Billing → Subscriptions
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Receive confirmation email

### Cancellation Timing
- Cancellation effective at end of current billing period
- You retain access until period end
- No refund for remaining time

### Reactivation
- Reactivate anytime from account settings
- Resume on next billing cycle
- Original billing date maintained
